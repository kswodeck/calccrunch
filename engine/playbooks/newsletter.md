# Playbook: Weekly Newsletter

Goal: bring subscribers back to the site (retention) in <2 min of reading. MailerLite,
1 email/week max (the promise on `/newsletter`). Draft into `engine/drafts/newsletter-YYYY-MM-DD.md`,
then paste into MailerLite and send. (Or set up MailerLite RSS automation off `/rss.xml`
to automate this entirely.)

## Template

**Subject** (≤45 chars, curiosity or utility): e.g. "New: convert anything in 1 click 🔄"

**Preview text** (≤90 chars): reinforce the subject, don't repeat it.

**Body**:
1. One-line friendly intro.
2. **🆕 New this week** — 1–3 new calculators, each: name (linked), one line on who it's for.
3. **💡 Tip of the week** — a genuinely useful tip tied to a calculator (link it).
4. **📖 Worth a read** — one blog post, linked, with a one-line hook.
5. Soft CTA: "Got a calculator you wish existed? Just reply." (replies = engagement + ideas).
6. Footer: unsubscribe (MailerLite auto), link to `/calculators`.

## Rules

- Every item links back to the site. Keep it skimmable (bullets, emoji headers).
- No more than ~200 words. Plain, warm tone — match the site's "no BS" voice.
- Always include the reply CTA — it's free user research and boosts deliverability.
